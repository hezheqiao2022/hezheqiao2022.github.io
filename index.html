Biography

I am a Ph.D. candidate at [Singapore Management University](https://www.smu.edu.sg/), supervised by [Dr. Guansong Pang](https://sites.google.com/site/gspangsite/home). Before that I obtained my M.S. degree and B.S. degree from [University of the Chinese Academy of Sciences](https://www.ucas.ac.cn/) and [Chengdu University of Technology](http://www.cdut.edu.cn/) in 2019 and 2022, respectively. My research interests are in graph representation learning and graph anomaly detection, with a special focus on unsupervised and weakly-supervised scenarios. Currently, I mainly focus on the research about how to detect anomalies on native datasets using unsupervised methods.

Prospective Ph.D., Master, and Undergraduate Students

I am looking for highly motivated students (PhD, master, undergraduate students) to work together on trustworthy deep learning on graph, especially pre-training, interpretability, generalization, and robustness, and their applications in real-world scenarios. Please feel free to send me your CV and transcripts, if you have interest. We are also actively looking for opportunities in research, partnership and commercialization in exciting data science projects.
