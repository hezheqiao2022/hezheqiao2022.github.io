Biography

I am a Ph.D. candidate at [Singapore Management University] , supervised by [Dr. Guansong Pang]. Before that I obtained my M.S. degree and B.S. degree from [University of the Chinese Academy of Sciences] and [Chengdu University of Technology] in 2019 and 2022, respectively. My research interests are in graph representation learning and graph anomaly detection, with a special focus on unsupervised and weakly-supervised scenarios. Currently, I mainly focus on the research about how to detect anomalies on native datasets using unsupervised methods.
